# Block Catalog

> All reusable email section blocks. Each block is a Jinja2 partial in `groww-creative-mcp/blocks/`.

---

## Core blocks (Phase 2)

### `header_logo`
Logo bar at top of email.
- **Params**: `brand` (groww/915), `logo_url`, `logo_alt`, `logo_width`
- **Variants**: Banner image (full-width) vs inline logo (centered, max-width 150px)
- **Seen in**: All 8 emails

### `hero_text`
Primary headline + optional subtitle and overline.
- **Params**: `headline` (supports inline HTML spans for colored words), `subtitle`, `overline` (uppercase label), `alignment` (center/left), `padding`
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 7, 8

### `hero_image`
Full-width or padded image.
- **Params**: `image_url`, `alt_text`, `width` (default 100%), `border_radius` (default 0), `padding`, `link_url`
- **Seen in**: Emails 2, 5, 6, 7, 8

### `article_section`
Heading + body paragraphs + optional bullet list.
- **Params**: `heading`, `paragraphs` (list of strings, supports inline HTML), `bullets` (list), `heading_align`, `body_align`, `padding`, `body_padding`
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 7, 8

### `factor_card`
Tinted background card with title + body text. Used for explaining contributing factors.
- **Params**: `title`, `body`, `bg_color` (default #E6FCF5), `border_radius` (default 12px), `text_align` (default center), `padding`
- **Note**: Production version has NO icons, NO sentiment coloring. Just plain mint card.
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 7, 8

### `cta_button`
Primary call-to-action button with VML Outlook fallback.
- **Params**: `text`, `url`, `style` (primary=green / blue / dark), `alignment` (center/left/full_width), `padding`, `border_radius` (default 10px)
- **Green**: #00b386 text white (default Groww)
- **Blue**: #5367ff text white (feature launches)
- **VML**: Bulletproof Outlook rendering via `<!--[if mso]>` conditional
- **Seen in**: All 8 emails

### `social_proof_banner`
"INDIA'S NO.1 STOCK BROKER" mint-green bar.
- **Params**: `title` (default "INDIA'S NO.1 STOCK BROKER"), `subtitle` (default "Loved and trusted by 1 Crore+ active investors"), `bg_color` (default #f4fdf9), `padding`
- **Seen in**: Emails 5, 6, 7, 8

### `divider`
Visual separator between sections.
- **Params**: `style` (line/space/dotted), `color` (default #f0f0f0), `margin`, `width`
- **Seen in**: Email 8

### `footer_standard`
Full email footer.
- **Params**: `brand` (groww/915), `show_social` (bool), `social_icon_urls` (dict), `show_sebi` (bool), `unsubscribe_url`, `registered_address`
- **Contains**: Social icon row, "Follow us" text, copyright, unsubscribe link, About Us | Terms | Privacy links, registered office address, SEBI disclaimer
- **Seen in**: All 8 emails

### `card_wrapper`
Wraps other blocks in a card container.
- **Params**: `shadow`, `border_radius`, `padding`, `background`, `content_blocks` (list of inner block defs)
- **Use case**: Commodity listing with multiple card groups
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 4

## Data blocks (Phase 3)

### `chart_image`
Hosted chart PNG with border-radius.
- **Params**: `image_url`, `alt_text`, `width` (default 100%), `border_radius` (default 16px), `padding`
- **Note**: The PNG is generated by chart-image-agent separately, then referenced by URL
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 8

### `step_instruction`
Numbered step with text + app screenshot image.
- **Params**: `steps` (list of {number, title, description, image_url, image_alt})
- **Seen in**: Emails 5, 6

### `contract_row`
Commodity/stock listing rows.
- **Params**: `items` (list of {icon, name, exchange, price, change_amount, change_pct, link_url}), `show_separator`, `separator_after_last`
- **Icons**: barrel, barrel_stacked, gold_bar, gas_flame, silver_bar
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 4

### `filter_pills`
Horizontal pill row with active state.
- **Params**: `pills` (list of strings), `active` (string)
- **Active style**: 1.5px solid #8b8b8b border, weight 500
- **Inactive style**: 1px solid token border_pill, weight 400
- **Padding**: 6px 12px (reduced from 18px to prevent overflow)
- **Seen in**: Emails 1, 4

### `listing_header`
Page title with back arrow and action link.
- **Params**: `title`, `show_back_arrow`, `action_text`, `action_icon`, `action_url`, `action_underline` (dashed)
- **Seen in**: Email 4
