# SEBI Regulations & Constraints

## Responsibility Charter (Dec 2023)

| Event | Date | Impact |
|-------|------|--------|
| Responsibility Charter discussions | Dec-23 | SEBI-aligned responsible broker policy |
| F&O cross-sell **paused** | Jan-24 | Stopped cross-selling F&O to non-F&O base |
| F&O cross-sell **restarted** | Mar-24 | ONLY for F&O-onboarded + F&O-affinity users |
| Activation funnel targeting | Mar-24 | Shifted to PA-based segmentation |

**Result**: F&O NTU share declining M/M, but not proportionally (organic conversions continue).

## Other Constraints

- **No "pre-apply" language** in IPO communications
- **MTF**: Same responsible broker framing applies
- **WA BIC pricing**: Increased 51p → 73p (anti-spam measure)
- **Email file size**: Max 102KB (Gmail clips beyond)
- **Domain warmup**: 30-day ramp, must maintain >20% open rate
