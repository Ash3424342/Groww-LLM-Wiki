# NTU — New Transacting User

User who transacted for the first time in a given period. Endpoint of the Activation funnel.

## ⚠️ Naming Collision

In **segment names** like `NTU_MF_Stock_LD`, NTU means **Non-TTU** (users who haven't reached TTU status). Two meanings — context determines which.
