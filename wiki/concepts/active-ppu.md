# Active PPU

**Formula**: COUNT(distinct active products per ATU) / COUNT(ATUs)

Replaced deprecated PPU (Products Transacted Ever / TTU) which fluctuated with market conditions. Active PPU is more stable and actionable.

**Product scope** (Oct 2023): Stocks (Intraday/cash), F&O, MF, Credit/Loans, UPI payments, BBPS, Insurance.
