# Source: Growth Vertical Overview

## Summary

Foundation document defining what the Growth (User Engagement) vertical does, how it's organized, and how campaigns flow from idea to measurement.

## Key Facts

- **Mission**: Drive activation (0→1 / [[fid]]) and retention (1→N / [[rid]]) across all Groww products
- **Structure**: 5 pods, 8 core people
- **Activation funnel**: 7-8 steps from Signup → [[fid]]
- **Personalization**: Generic (cohort) → ML-personalized (user-level)
- **Campaign pipeline**: Cohort → Content → [[audience-manager]] → Send → Measure
