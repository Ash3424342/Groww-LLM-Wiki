# Source: Vocabulary

## Summary

A-Z glossary of 60+ terms, acronyms, tag naming conventions, and segment notation used across the Growth vertical. Each entry marked with confidence status: ✅ confirmed, ⚠️ uncertain, ❓ unknown.

## What This Source Establishes

- Canonical definitions for every acronym (FID, RID, NTU, TTU, CNTU, etc.)
- Tag naming convention: `D{N}_{milestone}_from_{origin}`
- Segment naming: HD = High Difference, LD = Low Difference in affinity scores
- NTU naming collision: "New Transacting User" (metric) vs "Non-TTU" (segment prefix)
- Active PPU formula (replaced deprecated PPU)
- GCG sizing by lifecycle stage
