# WhatsApp Channel

High-engagement off-app channel. Managed by [[nikhil]] (channel manager) + [[yash]] (trading).

## Economics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| BIC (Business Initiated) | 73p/message (up from 51p) |
| UIC (User Initiated) | 33p/message |
| Industry frequency | 1/week (major brands), 2/week (Swiggy), 1/14D (fintech) |

## Experiment Results

| Use Case | Result |
|----------|--------|
| WA at Esign step | **+30% conversion**, ~200 Esigns/day, ₹145/conversion |
| WA at IFSC→BV step | +4% (too small, adds Rs.20 to CAC) → NOT live |
| Inactive non-OB reactivation | FID CAC = **1/5th** of remarketing |
| Inactive OB reactivation | FID CAC = **1/10th** of remarketing |
| Portfolio Update (TTU >60D inactive) | **+23% resurrection** (vs Email +2%) |
| WA vs Email resurrection gap | **~10-12x** (WA wins decisively) |
| WA resurrection cost | **Rs.75/user** |
| WA for transactions | Low (drives app opens, not transactions) |

## Key Insight

WA dramatically outperforms Email for resurrection. Email interacts with inactive users (40% open rate) but doesn't convert them back. WA does.

## Onboarding

WA live for 100% of users in onboarding journeys (via [[cosmos]]).
