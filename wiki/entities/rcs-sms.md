# RCS & SMS
**RCS** (Rich Communication Services) = enhanced SMS with images/buttons. **SMS** = fallback/transactional.
## Owner
[[aishwarya]] — owns both channels.
## Role
Used for push-unreachable users as an alternative to [[whatsapp-channel]]. Also used in onboarding Bank Verification drop-off journey (4 SMS + 2 RCS).
